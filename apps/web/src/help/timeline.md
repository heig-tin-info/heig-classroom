# Timeline

## What it shows

Each classroom unrolls into one lane per assignment, spanning from its start date to its deadline. The red line marks **now**. Assignments in progress glow with an accent ring; drafts are hollow, past or locked ones dimmed.

## How to navigate

- **Wheel or drag** — pan through past and future.
- **⌘/Ctrl + wheel** — zoom under the cursor (the buttons zoom too).
- **Crosshair button** — re-frame on what is in progress right now.
- **Chevron** on a classroom — collapse it back to a single overview track.

## How to open an assignment

Click its bar. Hovering shows the exact dates and state.
