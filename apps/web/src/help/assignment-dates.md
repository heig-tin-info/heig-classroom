# Start and deadline

## What they control

Students can accept and push between **Start** and **Deadline**. A reminder
e-mail goes out 24 h before the deadline; at the deadline the repositories are
locked or marked (see *At deadline*), the grade freezes after a grace period,
and the final review runs.

## Rescheduling

Both dates stay editable. Moving the deadline of an **expired** assignment
into the future reopens it: repositories are unlocked and grading resumes
until the new deadline.
