# Publication, start and deadline

## Two publication modes

**Manual** — the assignment goes live when you press *Publish*. The start is
the publication instant, and the deadline is either the **fixed date** you
picked, or **now + the duration** (days/hours) if you chose a duration. The
countdown starts at publication.

**Scheduled** — you set an absolute **start** and **deadline**; the platform
publishes the assignment automatically at the start date. No button to press.

## What the dates control

Students can accept and push between start and deadline. A reminder e-mail
goes out 24 h before the deadline; at the deadline the repositories are locked
or marked (see *At deadline*), the grade freezes after a grace period, and the
final review runs.

## Rescheduling

Dates stay editable. Moving the deadline of an **expired** assignment into the
future reopens it: repositories are unlocked and grading resumes until the new
deadline.
