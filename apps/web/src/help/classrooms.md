# My classrooms

## What is a classroom

A classroom binds a **GitHub organization** to a **student roster**. Assignments created in it are distributed as private repositories inside that organization, one per student.

## How to switch views

Use the buttons on the right: **cards**, a **sortable list** or the **timeline**. The search field filters as you type (fuzzy match on name and organization).

## How to open things

- Click a card to open the classroom.
- Click the organization name to open it on GitHub.
- Hover the student badges to see who is enrolled and who has already claimed their seat.

## How to see archived classrooms

Click the **archive box** button next to the view switches. Archived classrooms appear as muted cards with their archive date; **Restore** brings one back among the active classrooms. Archiving never deletes anything — data and GitHub repositories are kept.

## How to test the student experience

Open a classroom and use **Join as student** on the roster card: you take a *staff* seat (excluded from the headcount). The **graduation-cap** button in the header then switches to the real student view — accept the assignment, push, watch your grade.
